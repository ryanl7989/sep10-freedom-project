Architecture

Intro

Architecture is the art of designing and constructing structures. It involes creating spaces that are not only functional and sustainable but also beautiful and inovative. Architecture has been used for thousands of years for things like the pyramids and ancient temples. The importance of Architecture is immense as it can be used to enhance quality of life, express cultures, and boost economy. This site will be about the technological inventions of architecture now and in the future.

Existing Hardware

3D Scanner

Can be used when you need to document measurements and photographs.
Can make a 360 photograph of a space.
Good for complex spaces or projects far from home.

Laser Measurer

A tool that is used to measure a certain distance using a laser
Very accurate and easy to use.
Good for smaller projects

Laser Cutter

A laser cutter is a machine that uses a focused laser beam to cut materials such as wood, plastic, or metal
Has an onboard camera that shows the material on the cutting board.
Has software that allows you to move your cuts where you want

Adjustable Table

You can change from sitting to standing.
Good for stretching your legs and getting your blood flowing.
It can boost worker morale, efficiency, and productivity.

X-acto Knife

Helps you cut intricate details on designs
Good for creating models
Versitile with various shapes and sizes for different cutting needs.

Self Healing Cutting Mat

Made of flexible material that close up after each cut which prevent deep indents that can affect accuracy
Has grid lines that help with cutting
Protects the surface below and provides a non-slippery surface to work on.

AutoCAD

Sketch Up
