Intro

Architecture is the art of designing and constructing structures. It involes creating spaces that are not only functional and sustainable but also beautiful and inovative. Architecture has been used for thousands of years for things like the pyramids and ancient temples. The importance of Architecture is immense as it can be used to enhance quality of life, express cultures, and boost economy.
